How to Delete Your Account and Data

Your player profile in Who? is essential for saving your progress, scores, and any purchases you’ve made within the app. It also stores your preferences and gameplay history, and allows you to log in using your Google account (via OAuth).

Important: Deleting your account is permanent and cannot be undone.

When you delete your account:

  • All your progress (levels, scores, and achievements) will be lost;
  • Any purchased or earned items linked to your account will be permanently removed;
  • Your usage history and personal preferences will also be deleted from our servers.

How to Delete Your Account

You can request the deletion of your account directly from within the Who? app:

  1. Open the app and go to the Profile section;
  2. Tap “Delete my account”;
  3. Carefully read the information displayed and confirm your choice.

Once confirmed, all your data will be permanently deleted from our systems.
