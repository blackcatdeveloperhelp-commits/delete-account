How to Delete Your Account and Data

This page explains how users can request account and data deletion for the mobile game Who? Trivia Quiz Game, developed by Trick Cat Studio.

Your player profile in Who? is used to save progress, scores, achievements, purchases, preferences, and gameplay history. Authentication is performed using Google Sign-In (OAuth).

Important: Deleting your account is permanent and cannot be undone.

When you delete your account:

  • All progress (levels, scores, and achievements) will be permanently deleted;
  • All purchased or earned items will be removed;
  • All personal data, preferences, and usage history will be erased from our servers.

Delete Your Account from the App

You can delete your account directly within the Who? app:

  1. Open the app and go to the Profile section;
  2. Tap “Delete my account”;
  3. Read the information and confirm your request.

Alternative Account and Data Deletion Method

If you are unable to access the app, you may also request account and data deletion by contacting the developer directly.

Send an email to:
trickcatstudio@gmail.com
Subject: Account Deletion Request – Who?

Please include the email address associated with your account. All data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.
